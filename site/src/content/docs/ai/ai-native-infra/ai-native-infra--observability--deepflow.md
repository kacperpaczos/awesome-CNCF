## Opis

DeepFlow leverages eBPF to collect signals such as performance metrics, distributed tracing, and CPU & GPU profiling. Its goal is to provide zero-code observability for complex cloud-native and AI applications, particularly those involving LLM training and inference using frameworks like PyTorch.
