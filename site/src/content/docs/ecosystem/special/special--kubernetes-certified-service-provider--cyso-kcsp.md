## Opis

Cyso Cloud, based in Netherlands, operating in the EU, our Managed Kubernetes service helps you effortlessly manage, scale, and optimise your applications in secure and compliant way (ISO27K1/SOC2/DORA/GDPR) in both Private and Public Cloud environments.
