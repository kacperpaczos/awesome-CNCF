## Opis

Provides the ability to aggregate dispersed fault data into risk events, as well as emergency response, fault localization (root cause analysis, diagnostic decision trees), and fault handling (emergency self-healing, multi-active disaster recovery), to achieve a full set of emergency technology risk prevention and control capabilities in 1 minute discovery - 5 minutes localization - 10 minutes recovery.
