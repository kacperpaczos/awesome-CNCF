## Opis

Volcengine Managed Service for Prometheus (VMP) is a fully managed, maintenance-free cloud-native monitoring system that inherits and extends the open-source Prometheus ecosystem. The service delivers out-of-the-box observability data collection, alerting and actionable insights, enabling users to quickly establish end-to-end monitoring capabilities across Kubernetes, AI services, and multi-cloud environments.
