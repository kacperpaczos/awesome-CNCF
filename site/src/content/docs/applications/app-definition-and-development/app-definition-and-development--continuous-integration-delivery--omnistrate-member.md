## Opis

Omnistrate is a CoPilot for platform teams — helping them build software and agent platforms and turn cloud-native projects into production-ready  platforms without years of custom glue code. Instead of stitching together CI/CD, runtimes, infra-as-code, Day-2 operations, integrations, and  billing from scratch for every customer — and making it all work across clouds, regions, accounts, and deployment configurations — Omnistrate  provides ready-to-use modules that integrate directly with the CNCF ecosystem: Kubernetes, Argo, Flux, Helm, Operators, Kustomize, Terraform,  Prometheus, and more. By building on top of CNCF projects rather than reinventing them, Omnistrate enables modern software companies to deliver  applications in any model — OnPrem, BYOC, PaaS, SaaS, or Agent-aaS — in days, not years.
