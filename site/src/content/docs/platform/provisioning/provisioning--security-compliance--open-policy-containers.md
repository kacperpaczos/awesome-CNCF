## Opis

A docker-inspired CLI for building, tagging, pushing, pulling, and signing OPA policies to and from OCI-compliant registries.

## Use cases

- Open Policy Containers: A docker-inspired CLI for building, tagging, pushing, pulling, and signing OPA policies to and from OCI-compliant registries
- Skanowanie obrazów i runtime security w K8s
- Policy-as-code dla admission control
- CIS benchmarks i hardening klastrów
