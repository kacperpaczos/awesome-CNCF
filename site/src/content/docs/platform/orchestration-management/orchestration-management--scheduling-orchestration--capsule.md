## Opis

Capsule implements a multi-tenant and policy-based environment in your Kubernetes cluster. It is designed as a micro-services-based ecosystem with the minimalist approach, leveraging only on upstream Kubernetes.

## Use cases

- Capsule: Capsule implements a multi-tenant and policy-based environment in your Kubernetes cluster. It is designed as a micro-services-based ecosystem with the minimalist approach, leveraging only on upstream Kubernetes
- Orkiestracja kontenerów w skali produkcyjnej
- Autoscaling i self-healing workloadów
- Multi-tenant platformy wewnętrznego developer experience
- Zarządzanie cyklem życia aplikacji w klastrze
