## Opis

Cozystack is a free PaaS platform and framework for building private clouds and providing users/customers with managed Kubernetes,  KubeVirt-based VMs, databases as a service, NATS, message brokers, etc. with GPU support in VMs and Kubernetes clusters.

## Use cases

- Cozystack: Cozystack is a free PaaS platform and framework for building private clouds and providing users/customers with managed Kubernetes,  KubeVirt-based VMs, databases as a service, NATS, message brokers, etc. with GPU support in VMs and Kubernetes clusters
- Orkiestracja kontenerów w skali produkcyjnej
- Autoscaling i self-healing workloadów
- Multi-tenant platformy wewnętrznego developer experience
- Zarządzanie cyklem życia aplikacji w klastrze
