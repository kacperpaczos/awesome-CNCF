## Opis

Welkin is a security-hardened Kubernetes distribution. It is designed to meet the tough demands on security and compliance of regulated industries. The distribution includes logging, monitoring, single-sign-on integration with common identity providers, vulnerability scanning, intrusion detection, strict security hardening of the entire platform, and additional safeguards.
