## Opis

The plusserver Kubernetes Engine (PSKE) based on Gardener reduces the complexity in managing multi-cloud environments and enables companies to orchestrate their containers and cloud-native applications across a variety of platforms such as plusserver’s pluscloud open or hyperscalers such as AWS, either by mouseclick or via an API.
